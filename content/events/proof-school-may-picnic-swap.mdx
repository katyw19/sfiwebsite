Enjoy food, community, and clothes at the May Family Picnic. We'll be hosting a fun clothing swap alongside the picnic — bring items to donate and shop the swap for something new to you.

It's the perfect pairing: good weather, good food, and a chance to give your closet a sustainable refresh. Families and students of all ages are welcome.

**What to bring:** clean, gently-used clothing, shoes, and accessories.
