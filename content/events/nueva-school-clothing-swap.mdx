Trade your gently-used or unworn clothes for the outfits of your dreams. Our annual Nueva swap is a favorite — refresh your wardrobe without spending a dime.

Bring what you no longer wear and discover pieces someone else is ready to pass on. Every item that finds a new home is one less garment headed for the landfill.

**What to bring:** clean, gently-used clothing, shoes, and accessories.

_This event recurs annually. Want to bring a swap to your school? [Get in touch](/take-action)._
