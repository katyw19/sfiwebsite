Love free clothes! Join us during Earth Week for a community clothing swap. Bring gently-used items to donate, then shop from what everyone else brought. Let's celebrate Earth Day together.

This annual swap is one of our favorites — a chance to refresh your wardrobe, keep clothes out of the landfill, and connect with others who care about sustainable fashion. Bring a bag of gently-used clothing, and take home something new to you.

**What to bring:** clean, gently-used clothing, shoes, and accessories — no tears, rips, or stains.
